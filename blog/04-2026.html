Hi sorry if you though this journal was gonna be normal, it's not. My favorite song right now is "Banana Up My Ass" by ImEthan. Most write off the song as a shitpost at it is literally about a guy telling someone to put a banana up their ass. So why the hell is this my favorite song right now?


Recontextualization can suck. A lot. I've had a lot of mental breakdowns in the past as a lot of the way I think is based on beliefs. Beliefs are assumptions we choose and deem to be correct based upon our values and interpretation of the world. So, when something you believed changes, many other thoughts, thinking patterns or beliefs based upon this original belief require reinspection. That shit is overwhelming. Recently I was really hoping for a positive recontextualization, one that would mean I didn't have to think about certain things. I did not undergo this recontextualization, instead being promoted by a different one that has caused me a lot of grief. I am in the process of dealing with this heavy onslaught of emotions (especially gender dysphoria) and being very honest I am struggling to find meaning in my life.


So where does this song come in? At this point there might as well be a banana up my ass. Such an absurd thing yet this is where the comfort lies: in absurdism. Living in this world is absurd, being subject to these emotions is absurd. Having to recompile my psyche means statements such as having a banana up my anal canal literally sounds normal compared to everything else. With a statement such as stick a banana straight up my ass, it demands attention and brings me back to reality as I am no longer questioning fundamentals of my way of being and instead brought to the simple unrealistic idea of the banana being in an ass.


Sexual themes in songs have always made me uncomfortable and ironically when it's too such an extreme and only to that extreme, that's where I can find comfort in them (because of the absurdism). Examples of songs I love that share this theme are Knot Song, PUPPYPLAY!, femboifoxxx, and PREY by passengerprincess. Yes it's all furry music. Next question.
